About

My name is Amanda WouldGo. I do lots of things to make a living, but one thing I do is make art.

Papercut Monsters is a place for me to display the things I've made, as well as thoughts on what to make next.

What is papercutting?

Papercutting is like making a stencil by cutting images out of a piece of paper. I like to keep in mind the ideas of positive and negative space.
