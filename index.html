What is papercutting?

Papercutting is like making a stencil by cutting images out of a piece of paper. I like to keep in mind the ideas of positive and negative space.

-Look at Elsita's excellent example of a papercutting website-

Some elements that make Elsita's site so great are:

  1. inline menu at top
  2. there are blocks next to one another(I may be using the wrong terminology here).
  3. there is one common image at the top of each page
  4. her top menu is animated upon scroll-over
  5. light whitish or super pale grey background
  6. lots of whitespace, which makes it readable
  7. everything is centered (I'm excited to learn more about bootstrap and css so to enhance my site's sense of alignment)
